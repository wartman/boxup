Blocks

Blocks are what everything in Boxup are built out of. A block is simple: it consists of a block header -- a block name and (potentially) properties inside square brackets (e.g. 

[Foo bar=bin]
or just 
[Foo]
) -- and block children, which may be properties, text, other blocks or a combination of all of these.

Comments

Comments are written like this: 

[/ Comment /]
. They can come anywhere in a Boxup document. They should be nestable soon too (although they aren't quite yet).
